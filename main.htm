Where history and modernity come together!

Our history club has been serving the community since 2003. Our goal is to teach the community about history, and to make it fun while we're at it. We primarily focus on medieval, classical, and the early modern period.

We are dedicated to bringing history to the modern person. We do this by offering a varity of services and products, such as:

  • Weekly group meetings
  • Community outreach programs
  • Seminars (see Seminars page)
  • Product shop for all things history (see Products page)
