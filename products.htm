Journals and Writing Supplies

We have blank journals available so you can write whatever you want, and we also have professional writing supply kits.

Maps and Cartography Supplies

Our collection of historical maps is vast, contact us so we can find the right one for you. We also have professional cartography supplies if you wish to make your own.

Children's History Books

Not all history is family-friendly, but our children's books are! We have the For Kids and Captivating Stories book collections in stock.

Classical and Post-Classical History Books

Our classical history books cover topics such as Ancient Greece, Achaemenid Persia, Egypt, Mesopotamia, and the most fun of all - Alexander the Great! Our post-classical collection mostly deals with the Roman Republic and later the Roman Empire.

Medieval History Books

Our medieval history books focus on feudal Europe and the Arab world. They cover things like the Hundred Years War, the Holy Roman Empire, the Crusades, and the Islamic Caliphate.

Early Modern History Books

Our early modern period history books primarily focus on the colonial powers of France, England, Spain, and the Netherlands, but also cover areas in Asia like India and China. You can find books that cover the New World as well, such as the Aztecs or the Inca.
